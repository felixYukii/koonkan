Scan Series 2
(aka Voyage to the Betwixt-and-Between)


In this series of creative digital photocollages, I tried to visually represent the dilemma that Southeast Asia currently faces. My research interest grew when a territorial dispute over the South China Sea, one of the busiest waterways in the world, garnered the attention of world mass media over the past years.
